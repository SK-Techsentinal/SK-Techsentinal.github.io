Everything You Need
to Know

We want your day at Pine City Zoo to be unforgettable. Here's everything to help you prepare.

🕐
09:00 – 17:00

Opening Hours

Open 7 days a week including public holidays. Last entry at 16:00. Extended summer hours: open until 19:00 Fridays.

📍

Location

14 Pine Avenue, Randburg, Johannesburg, 2194. Ample free parking available. Accessible by Gautrain bus route 7.

🍽️

Dining

Three dining options on-site: The Savanna Restaurant, Jungle Bites Café, and Arctic Ice Cream Bar. Picnic areas available.

♿

Accessibility

Fully wheelchair accessible with paved paths throughout. Wheelchairs available to hire at the main entrance at no cost.
